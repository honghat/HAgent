## Prompt templates cho từng vibe

### Scene "nhớ nhung/cô đơn"
```
A cute stick figure man standing alone, looking into the distance,
holding a phone in his hand, soft sky with gentle clouds and sunlight,
flat vector illustration, simple minimal style, white background, warm atmosphere
```

## Common prompt keywords cho stick figure love theme
- `pink warm background` / `soft warm colors`
- `glowing red heart`
- `sunset orange purple`
- `flat vector illustration, simple minimal style`
- `love atmosphere`
- `white background` (giúp ChatGPT xoá nền tạp)
- `cute stick figure` / `stick figure man`
